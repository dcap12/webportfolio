About Me

Fashion Marketing and Public Relations

My name is Danielle Caprarola, and I am a marketing professional specializing in fashion marketing and public relations. With a degree in digital media, I have many skills in digital marketing and graphic design, and have been working in the fashion industry for over two years. I have extensive knowledge on fashion trends, styling, marketing, and public relations writing, and my skills allow me to excel in marketing and fashion related job positions.

In this portfolio, I have included some images of old marketing projects I worked on, highlighting key parts of some of the campaigns and my skills in the marketing industry. I have also included samples of my public relations writing, as well as photos of some of the graphic designs I have done for digital media and marketing purposes.
